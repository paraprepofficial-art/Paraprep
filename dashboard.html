ParaPrep solves all of that — built specifically for paramedical students.

Privacy Policy
ParaPrep  ·  Effective: March 2026  ·  v1.0
✅ ParaPrep does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Everything stays on your device.
1. Introduction
ParaPrep is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we handle, how it is stored, and your rights. Because ParaPrep is a website (no server, no backend), your data never leaves your device.
2. Information We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect, store, or transmit:
  • Your name, email address, or phone number (even if entered in the optional profile)
  • Your quiz scores, history, or performance data
  • Your bookmarks or saved questions
  • Your IP address, location, or device identifiers
  • Any usage analytics or behavioural tracking data
  • Cookies — ParaPrep uses localStorage only, not cookies
3. Data Stored on Your Device
ParaPrep stores the following data locally in your browser's localStorage — only on your device:
  • Profile data — optional name, email, mobile, and a SHA-256 hashed password (if you create an account)
  • Quiz history — your past attempt results (score, date, mode)
  • Bookmarks — questions you have saved for revision
  • Settings — dark mode preference, timer settings, font size
  • Study streak — daily challenge completion data
  • Seen questions — which questions you have already been shown
This data is stored entirely on your device and is never transmitted anywhere. You can delete all of it at any time from Settings → Clear All Data.
4. Passwords & Security
If you create a local account, your password is hashed using SHA-256 (via the browser's Web Crypto API) before being stored. We never store plain-text passwords. However, since all data is stored locally in your browser, we strongly recommend:
  • Not sharing your device with others while logged in
  • Using a unique password not used on other services
  • Logging out on shared or public devices
5. Third-Party Services
ParaPrep loads fonts from Google Fonts (Outfit and DM Sans) on first load. This is the only external network request made by the app. Google may collect standard request data (IP address, browser type) as part of their font delivery service. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details. After the first load, the app works fully offline with no further external requests.
6. Bug Reports
If you submit a bug report using the in-app "Report a Bug" form, the report is sent via Gmail to paraprep.official@gmail.com. The report may contain the information you voluntarily type (bug description, section, question number). We use this information only to fix the reported issue and do not share it with any third parties.
7. Children's Privacy
ParaPrep is designed for students aged 17 and above who are preparing for paramedical competitive exams. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
8. Your Rights & Data Control
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:
  • View your data — all data is visible in your browser's DevTools → Application → localStorage
  • Delete your data — go to Settings → Clear All Data to wipe everything
  • Export — your browser's localStorage can be accessed directly at any time
There is no account to delete from our side because we hold no data.
