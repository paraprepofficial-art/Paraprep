Top 20 High-Yield Optometry Topics for AIIMS CRE

📅 March 2026 ⏱ 7 min read ✍️ ParaPrep Team

Based on PYQ analysis, these 20 Optometry topics repeat most frequently in AIIMS CRE. Mastering them can earn you 12–16 marks in the technical sections.

Cornea & External Eye

  • Layers of cornea — 5 layers: Epithelium, Bowman's, Stroma, Descemet's, Endothelium
  • Corneal thickness — Central ~550 µm, peripheral ~700 µm
  • Keratoconus — Progressive ectasia, Vogt's striae, Fleischer ring, Munson's sign
  • HSV keratitis — Dendritic ulcer, geographic ulcer, treatment with acyclovir

Retina & Posterior Segment

  • Layers of retina — 10 layers; RPE is the outermost, NFL innermost
  • Macula anatomy — Fovea (best vision), foveola, umbo, foveal avascular zone (FAZ)
  • Diabetic retinopathy — NPDR (microaneurysms, dot haemorrhages) vs PDR (neovascularisation)
  • Retinal detachment — Rhegmatogenous (break), tractional (fibrous), exudative (fluid)
  • ARMD — Dry (drusen, RPE atrophy) vs Wet (CNV, sudden vision loss)

Glaucoma

  • Normal IOP — 10–21 mmHg (Goldmann applanation tonometry)
  • Cup-disc ratio — Normal ≤0.5; >0.6 suspicious; asymmetry >0.2 significant
  • POAG vs PACG — Open vs closed angle; PACG causes acute pain and redness
  • Visual field defects — Arcuate scotoma, nasal step, altitudinal defect

Refraction & Optics

  • Myopia — Image in front of retina, concave (−) lens corrects
  • Hypermetropia — Image behind retina, convex (+) lens corrects
  • Astigmatism — Cylindrical lens; WTR vs ATR vs oblique
  • Presbyopia — Loss of accommodation after 40 years, +1.00 add at 40 yrs
  • Snellen chart — 6/6 = normal; minimum angle of resolution = 1 arcmin

Lens & Cataract

  • Cataract types — Nuclear (oldest, brown), cortical (spoke-wheel), PSC (posterior subcapsular — worst glare)
  • Surgical methods — PHACO (small incision) vs ECCE vs ICCE
  • IOL power formula — SRK/T formula; axial length by A-scan

Ocular Motility

  • EOM nerve supply — CN III (MR, IR, SR, IO, LPS), CN IV (SO), CN VI (LR)
  • Cover test — Detects manifest strabismus; alternating cover test for latent
  • Amblyopia — Lazy eye; treatment before 7 years with patching of good eye
